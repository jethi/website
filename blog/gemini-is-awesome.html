Gemini is Awesome


21 Oct, 2020 by Arsh Jethi


So, what is Gemini ?


Gemini is a protocol just like HTTP and Gopher. Provides more features than Gopher and less features than HTTP. Focus on simplicity, privacy and generality. I strongly recommend reading more about it at the official source.


Why I like it ?


I discovered Gemini yesterday and the most exciting thing for me was how simple it was to understand the working under the hood.


A working client in 100 lines of code and a TLS library ? It is amazing for introducing students to an internet protocol.
